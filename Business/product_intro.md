ShipReq is a modern, web-based requirements development and management tool, currently in development
It aims to boost the quality of requirements and therefore the end-product.
It aims to make the requirements process easier, faster, and ultimately a joy rather than a chore.

ShipReq is a response to frustration of failed projects, disappointment of clients and developers.
ShipReq is born from a desire to change peoples' lives and raise the quality of software.

Being in its early stages, ShipReq currently focuses exclusively on relieving the pain involved with use cases.
Writing use cases in ShipReq, an author no longer has to manually manage step numbering or check that references to
steps are correct when new steps are added. Authors are free to concentrate on content, the business requirements,
leaving ShipReq to take care of details and provide live feedback.
ShipReq will even maintain a real-time diagram of possible paths through the use case,
a superbly useful visualisation often omitted due to effort, with no additional effort required of the author.

Many exciting features are planned.
