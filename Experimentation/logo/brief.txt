Logo for new online tech product
================================

We are a small startup for an online web application. In the engineering and software worlds, teams will normally write, analyse and document requirements before they start creating their product, be it a website, an elevator or a financial trading platform. This is synonymous to an architect designing and refining blueprints before building a house. Our product improves quality and reduces cost to clients.

The name of our product is "ShipReq". This is because when a development product is completed, it is normally "shipped" to clients, and our product focuses on the Requirements Phase. "Ship Requirements", "ShipReq".
(We don't have a finalised tagline, but it will likely be along the lines of "Ship Your Requirements Fast" or something that sounds less rubbish.)

ShipReq is a play on words. It can also be interpreted as "shipwreck". Therefore, logos may use this interpretation, a nautical theme, or similar. You are also free to disregard this meaning entirely and focus something relevant on the product's function.

Feel free to include or not include the text "ShipReq" in the Logo. We'll be interested to see what works.

Our philosophy for the product (in order of priority) is that it is
1. Useful
2. Joyful
3. Easy
4. Simple
5. Powerful

Logo feeling brainstorm:
- confident
- slick
- capable
- adept
- minimal
- clean
- lean
- uncluttered
- appealing
- productive
- cute
- attractive
- impressive
- trustworthy
- memorable
- modern

Should not be:
- overly corporate
- soulless
- lifeless
- overwhelming
- busy
- too detailed

Target audience would be any business, consultants or freelancers that develop a product, primarily software development teams.

Logo must
* be easily resizable - must look good big and small
* in vector graphic format, preferably svg

Logo must not
* just be stylised font/text - some kind of visual is required.

Nice to have:
* looks good in black and white
* looks good and easily identifiable when resized down to 1-inch.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Having looked through all the excellent submissions there are a few clear design preferences that have emerged in our minds.

We don't like just a stylised 'S'. Please avoid basing the visual on any letter in the alphabet.
The text shouldn't consume more than half the logo.
We are disliking logos based solely on ships' sails. They seem to convey a feeling of passiveness, being stranded, dependent on winds. We'd prefer to convey activeness, the power to drive and take control. You are free to ignore this aspect, but please avoid passive implications.

Overall we've seen some great designs and are thoroughly enjoying your creativity and artistic talent. Please keep it coming! Thanks.

The next update!
Thank you everyone for your wonderful designs. The creativity of the design submissions continues to astound us. Apologies for the lack of activity over the Christmas/New Year break.

We're going to extend the project deadline due to the Christmas break.

Despite some of the beautiful logos we have seen depicting ships, we have decided against this theme. We're discovering that it doesn't gel with the vision we have for our product. We're also ruling out abstract designs. While there have been some nice ones, they do not compare to the themed logos.

We have a much clearer image in our head of what we do and don't like. Thus, we ask that all future designs are based on one of the following:

Lighthouse
  there must be a light shining out. All other details are up to your creativity!
Anchor
  just a plain anchor will not work. It will need to be more interesting and/or impressionable

We look forward to seeing more submissions.
